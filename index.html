Instructions

  • Press Z to shoot LEFT
  • Press X to shoot UP
  • Press C to shoot RIGHT
  • Press the left arrow (<) to move the tower LEFT
  • Press the right arrow (>) to move the tower RIGHT

Objective

Shoot the falling toppings/pizzas and recieve points. Move left or right to avoid the objects, otherwise each object will do 10% damage. After 10 times it's game over man!
